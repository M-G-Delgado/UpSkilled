Pricing & Payments

Our workshops range from €45–450 (excl. VAT) depending on difficulty and duration. Discovery Courses start at €45 for quick 1-hour skills, while Multi-Day Intensives can reach €450 for 2–5 day immersive training. See our Course Catalogue for exact pricing.

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers, with all transactions secured using industry-standard encryption.

Charges occur at registration or upon reservation confirmation, typically 24 hours before the workshop begins. Visit our Course Catalogue for scheduling details.

Workshops range from 1-hour Discovery Courses to 5-day Multi-Day Intensives. Check our Course Catalogue for specific durations, like “Intermediate Carpentry” (3 hours) or “Intensive Auto Repair Certification” (5 days).
