Partner with UpSkilled

UpSkilled is a hands-on workshop marketplace designed to connect eager learners with local businesses and expert instructors. We use a 70% practice, 30% demo approach—plus gamification, multi-day intensives, and industry-specific curriculum—to deliver transformative, real-world skill building. By hosting your courses on UpSkilled, you can reach new audiences, boost your revenue, and join our mission to reshape how people learn practical skills.

Our flexible pricing structure spans from quick 1-hour Discovery courses to multi-day Intensives, ensuring every learner—beginner or advanced—can find exactly what they need. You keep 75–90% of each booking, with performance-based commission incentives that drop from 25% to as low as 10% for high performers. We handle marketing, booking, and payment processing, so you can focus on delivering top-quality workshops.
