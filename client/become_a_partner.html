UpSkilled is a hands-on workshop marketplace designed to connect eager learners with local businesses and expert instructors. By hosting your courses on UpSkilled, you can reach new audiences, boost your revenue, and join our mission to reshape how people learn practical skills.

You keep 75–90% of each booking, with performance-based commission incentives that drop from 25% to as low as 10% for high performers. We handle marketing, booking, and payment processing, so you can focus on delivering top-quality workshops.
